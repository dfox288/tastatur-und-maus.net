title: "What is the demoscene?"
body: "The demoscene is a subculture of programmers, musicians and visual artists who create 'demos' — real-time audiovisual works that run entirely on the hardware they're made for, with no prerendered video. Born on Commodore 64s and Amigas in the 1980s, it has thrived across every generation of hardware since. Demos are made to be shown at parties — and judged by the people who made them."
